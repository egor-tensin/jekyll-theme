Today, we’ll “prove” that \(2 \times 2 \ne 5\).

\[\begin{gather} 2 \times 2 = 2 + 2 = 4 \\ 4 \ne 5 \implies 2 \times 2 \ne 5 \end{gather}\]

Q.E.D.
